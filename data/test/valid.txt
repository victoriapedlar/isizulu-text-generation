Besingcwaba indodakazi yomfowethu eshone kade sihlaselwe ngabantu lapha. Manje unina wayeyilahle incane, yakhulela lapha kithi. Simangele uma efika namaphoyisa bezosephuca isidumbu. Sizamile ukubuza kodwa amaphoyisa athi athole umyalelo wezikhulu zawo. Sigcine singcwaba izimpahla zakhe ngoba phela umgodi uma usumbiwe ngeke usasala kanjalo,"" kusho uMthethwa ngokubalisa. Unina wengane, uNksz Nozipho Dube, uthe uyilandile ingane yakhe ngoba bebefuna ukuyingcwaba isinyelela yena engazi, ekubeni yena ubehlela ukuzingcwabela. Uthe ingane ibihlala kwaMthethwa kodwa ibingahlawuliwe, okwenza ukuthi ngokosiko kumele ingcwatshwe ekhaya konina. "Ngithenge ukudla komngcwabo nembuzi, salungisa konke kodwa sizwe kuthiwa kunenkonzo yomngcwabo kubo ka-Olwethu.
Samangala ukuthi kwenzenjani.
Sibe sesibikela amaphoyisa ukuthi akulandwe isidumbu.
Asithathile asifaka emakhazeni kahulumeni ngoba kusenombango oqhubekayo,"" kusho uNksz Dube.
Uthe okunye okumdidayo yindlela ingane eshone ngayo.
Kuqale kwathiwa idutshuliwe, kwase kuthiwa iklinyiwe.
Ngesikhathi elanda udaba uthe uselufake kubameli bakhe ukuze bamsize athole imvume yokungcwaba ingane yakhe Okhulumela amaphoyisa KwaZulu-Natal, uLieutenant Nqobile Gwala akakuchithanga ukuthi amaphoyisa asilandile isidumbu, uthe akukhulunywe namalungu omndeni othintekayo.
Umengameli waseNigeria, uMnuz Muhammadu Buhari, uthi kumele kube nomngcwabo owodwa omkhulu wabo bonke abafe kuqhuma imboni yokucwenga amafutha engekho emthethweni.
Uthi abantu bashe kabi kangangokuba akulula ukukhomba ukuthi bangobani.
