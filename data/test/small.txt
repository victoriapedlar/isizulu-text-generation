Uhulumeni uhlalaenxusa umphakathi kufanele ubambisane namaphoyisa ukulwa nobugebengu kodwa uma sekubulawa abazama ukulekelela abomthetho, lokhu kuzofaka itwetwe emphakathini. UNdunankulu waKwaZulu-Natal uNkk Nomusa Dube-Ncube, ngesikhathi ehambele inkonzo yomngcwabo kaMthethwa, ukhulume kabanzi ngenkinga yobugebengu emngceleni.
Sikubeke kwacaca ukuthi ubugebengu sizobhekana nabomahlanze, kufanele kucace ukuthi yithi esiphumelelayo noma yizigebengu, kusho uNkk Dube-Ncube.
Uma elakuleli lingomakhelwane futhi linobudlelwano neMozambique, yini evimbile ukuthi amazwe ahlangane abambisane aqede ubugebengu bokweqiswa kwezimoto emngceleni.
