Umengameli Cyril Ramaphosa utusa abantu bakuleli abayishaye indiva imibhikisho ebihlelwe ezweni lonke yi-EFF, bakhetha ukuhlala emakhaya.
Noma engazange aligagule ngegama iqembu kodwa uthe noma kukhona abebefuna ukuphoqa abantu bakuleli ukuthi babhikishe, bebasabisa kodwa ukujabulele ukuthi izakhamuzi azizange zibe yingxenye yemibhikisho.
Ngijabule ukuthi abantu abazange balisabele kuleli khwelo kodwa bazihlalele emakhaya basebenzisa ilungelo labo, kusho uRamaphosa izolo, ngesikhathi kugujwa uSuku lwamaLungelo Abantu eDe Aar, eNorthern Cape.
URamaphosa ubekhuluma ngemva kokuba i-EFF imashe ezindaweni ezehlukene ngoMsombuluko, ifuna ehle esikhundleni ngoba ikhala ngezinto eziningi okubalwa nokucishwa kukagesi kuleli.
Uthembise abantu ukuthi bazoqhubeka bathole usizo olubafanele. Wathi abantu banelungelo lokuthola izindlu, amanzi, ezempilo, imfundo esezingeni, ukuvikelwa, amanzi nezinye izidingo.
Uvumile ukuthi omasipala abaningi bayahluleka ukuhlinzeka izidingo zamanzi, izindlu nezinye emiphakathini ngenxa yokuthi kwabanye abekho abanamava okwenza lo msebenzi.
Kulo nyaka sihlela ukuphothula uhlelo oluthinta ukuxhaswa kwezitshudeni ezizalwa ngabantu abahola imali engaphezu kweyisikalo esibekwe yiNSFAS. 
Emasontweni edlule sibone ezinye izitshudeni zibhikisha zikhala ngezinto ezithinta izindawo zokuhlala ezikhungweni zemfundo kanjalo nemali yokuphila.
Amanye amaqembu okuyiPAC ne-Action SA, agubhe lolu suku eSharpeville, lapho kwashona khona abantu abangu-69 ababulawa amaphoyisa ombuso wobandlululo ngo-1960, ngesikhathi bebhikishela ukuphathwa kwamapasi.
Ungqongqoshe wezamaPhoyisa, uMnuz Bheki Cele, uthi bazobhekana nengalo yomthetho abazokwephula imithetho uma sekuqhubeka ukhukhulelangoqo wombhikisho ohlelwa yi-EFF.
UCele uthe amaphoyisa azophuma ngezinkani ukuqinisekisa ukuthi alukho udlame ngoMsombuluko. Ukusho lokhu esithangamini nabezindaba ebesibizwe ongqongqoshe abasezinhlakeni zokuphepha.
Ongqongqoshe ababhekene nezokuphepha ezweni baqinisekise abantu bakuleli ukuthi bazokube bevikelekile kunoma iyiphi inhlobo yodlame nokuphazamiseka okungase kuhambisane nombhikisho.
Baqhube baqinisekise ukuthi umbhikisho we-EFF ngeke ufane nezibhelu zangoJulayi 2021, lapho kwacekelwa khona phansi impahla, kwashona nenqwaba yabantu.
Senze izinhlelo zokuthi wonke umuntu ofuna ukuya emsebenzini ngoMsombuluko, abafuna ukuvakasha nabafuna ukuvula amabhizinisi abo, bakwazi ukukwenza lokho ngaphansi kwesimo esiphephile. Sifisa ukuqinisekisa abantu basemazweni angaphandle ukuthi akusilona iqiniso ukuthi amachweba nemingcele yakuleli kuzokube kuvaliwe.
Noma ngubani ozozama ukusabisa, ukuvimba abantu ukuthi baye emsebenzini noma ozozama ukuvala imigwaqo, uzobhekana nengalo yomthetho, kugcizelela uCele.
UCele uqhube wathi bazobambisana nezinkampani zonogada nezinhlangano zemiphakathi ezilwa nobugebengu ukubhekana nemibhikisho yangoMsombuluko.
Ungqongqoshe wezokuVikela, uNkk Thandi Modise, uthe amasosha akulungele ukwelekelela uma kunesidingo. Uthe bazothathelwa izinyathelo abagqugquzela udlame ezinkundleni zokuxhumana.
Elinye iphutha eselivumela ukuthi lenzeke yilile lokungathatheli izinyathelo abantu abagqugquzela udlame ezinkundleni zokuxhumana. Sesifundile kulelo phutha. Yingakho abantu bezothathelwa izinyathelo kulokhu, kuphawula uNkk Modise.
Ungqongqoshe ehhovisi likamengameli, uNksz Khumbudzo Ntshavheni, uthe ngeke bavume ukuthi kuphinde okwenzeka ngoJulayi 2021. Uthe uma i-EFF ifuna ukukhipha uRamaphosa esikhundleni, ayilinde ukhetho.
Ukhetho lungonyaka ozayo. Abantu kufanele bawine ukhetho uma befuna ukukhipha uhulumeni osemandleni njengamanje. Kuleli zwe amandla oMbuso awathathwa ngendluzula. Ngeke sikuvumele ukuthi kube khona abantu abazofuna ukuthatha amandla oMbuso ngendluzula, kuxwayisa uNksz Ntshavheni.
Iningizimu Afrika neMozambique, amazwe angomakhelwane ayahwebelana futhi aseqoqweni elilodwa lamazwe angu-16, iSouthern African Development Community (SADC). 
Isidingidwe kakhulu inkinga yobugebengu bokwebiwa kwezimoto kuleli zeqiswe umngcele ziyiswe eMozambique, ngasohlangothini lwakoManguzi nakoMhlabuyalingana, esifundeni uMkhanyakude. 
Kubikwa ukuthi uma imoto ike yawela umngcelewezwe yangena kwelinye kuba nzima ukuyithola ngoba kuyaphazamiseka ukusebenza kobuchwepheshe bokulandela imoto. 
Okunye wukuthi izimoto azithathwa nje ezindaweni ezisondelene nomngcele kuphela kodwa nakwezinye izindawo seziyathathwa okusho ukuthi iyadlondlobala inkinga. 
Kuthe kusabatshazwa indaba kaMthethwa, ngasekuqaleni kwale nyanga kwabulawa uMnuz Sandile Tembe, wasebukhosini bakwaTembe, okuthiwa ubeyilungu eliqavile leManguzi Policing Forum, elwa nokuthathwa kwezimoto. 
Ngaphandle kokubulawa kwezishoshovu ezilwa nobugebengu kodwa kunezigameko zokushiswa kwezimoto ezisuka eMozambique, zize kuleli ngenxa yokudinwa kwabantu bakuleli. 
Ukushiswa kwezimoto zabasuke bevakashile kuzolimaza umkhakha wezokuvakasha oyingxenye yomnotho ngoba kusho ukuthi izivakashi zaseMozambique, zizoyeka ukuza kuleli.
Uhulumeni uhlalaenxusa umphakathi kufanele ubambisane namaphoyisa ukulwa nobugebengu kodwa uma sekubulawa abazama ukulekelela abomthetho, lokhu kuzofaka itwetwe emphakathini. UNdunankulu waKwaZulu-Natal uNkk Nomusa Dube-Ncube, ngesikhathi ehambele inkonzo yomngcwabo kaMthethwa, ukhulume kabanzi ngenkinga yobugebengu emngceleni.
Sikubeke kwacaca ukuthi ubugebengu sizobhekana nabomahlanze, kufanele kucace ukuthi yithi esiphumelelayo noma yizigebengu, kusho uNkk Dube-Ncube.
Uma elakuleli lingomakhelwane futhi linobudlelwano neMozambique, yini evimbile ukuthi amazwe ahlangane abambisane aqede ubugebengu bokweqiswa kwezimoto emngceleni.
Umengameli wezwe uMnuz Cyril Ramaphosa uthe njengoba abantu bebhuqwa yindlala futhi nezinga lokungasebenzi liphezulu, bayaqhubeka nokubheka izindlela zokuqinisekisa ukuthi bayabaseka labo abadonsa kanzima.
Uthe yingakho isibonelelo sabangatohozi besilule kuze kube uMashi wonyaka ozayo, athemba ukuthi bazobe sebezitholile ezinye izindlela zokuqinisekisa ukuthi abathembele kuhulumeni, bayaqhubeka nokuthola usizo olufanele.
Odabeni oluthinta ukucinywa kukagesi okuphazamisa izinhlelo nabantu, uthe yingakho baqoka uNgqongqoshe obhekele ugesi, ukuze kuxazululwe le nkinga.
USolwazi Bonke Dumisa ongungoti wezomnotho nezomthetho, nguye obeqagele ukuthi iSarb izonyusa imali mboleko ngo-50 basis points ngesikhathi abanye ozakwabo bethi izonyuka ngo-25 basis points.
Akayifihli eyokuncitisana nonozinti besikimu esikhulu sengathi ubanga nabo ijezi kanti likhona ithemba lokuthi ekugcineni uzodlala esikimini esikhulu, konke sekulele kuyena.
